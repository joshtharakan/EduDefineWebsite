About us

Who we are

Edudefine is trying to bring educational institutions to an international attire by introducing innovative educational products.

Company Overview

EduDefine provides quality educational products across the globe.The company's value proposition is in their ability to leverage strategic partnerships with global educational compaies for the benifit of their clients.

Our Mission

To engage collaboratively with educational institutions,deliver innovative products and services that optimize the goals of the institutions,thus enabling them to focus on their key educational goals thus becoming a Globally admired Educational Company.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be a world-class educational services company that helps educational institutions achieving their goals more effectively yet effortlessly.We will accomplish our vision through our commitment to strategic growth,service par excellence and active community participation.
